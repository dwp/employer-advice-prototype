Policies and procedures for your business

The types of policies and procedures you have in place will depend on the size and function of your business.

The law

There are three policies that businesses are required to have. These are:

  • Disciplinary and dismissal
  • Grievance
  • Health and safety (if you have more than five employees)

As a minimum, to protect the business and your employees, you should also consider having the following policies:

  • Equality, diversity and inclusion
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), which covers the management and processing of personal data

Benefits of implementing policies and procedures

There can be several benefits to having workplace policies in place, including:

  • better employee retention
  • employees know what is expected of them
  • increased employee productivity
  • Increased employee morale
  • protection from issues that could result in failure of employment legislation

Having policies in place could help you in managing a complaint relating to disability discrimination or disability victimisation. You should also seek guidance from a legal professional if needed.
