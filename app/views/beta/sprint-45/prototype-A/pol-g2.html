Putting additional policy and procedures in place

There are other policies and procedures that could support your business and employees.

Recruitment policy

A recruitment policy should cover:

  • encouraging the recruitment of employees with health conditions or disabilities
  • that reasonable adjustments are considered at all stages of the recruitment process
  • that you will hire the best person for the job based on their application, assessments, and interview, and not to fill a quota

Dignity at work policy

A Dignity at work policy will make sure that employees are treated in a consistent and fair manner.

Sickness absence procedure

A Sickness absence procedure will support employees to maintain an acceptable level of attendance so they can carry out their work.
