Learn about your employee’s condition

You do not have to know the details of your employee’s condition before you talk to them.

However, knowing how your employee might be affected can help to prepare for your conversation with them and any changes at work they might need.

The NHS Health A to Z has details of many conditions and the most common symptoms.

Mental health

If your employee has a mental health condition it’s especially important to be patient to be patient and let them lead the conversation.

Physical conditions like back pain or a disability can effect mental health. They can cause stress, anxiety and affect everyday life.

Mental health conditions caused by something outside of work can affect someone’s ability to do their job.

The NHS mental health website has advice to help you support someone facing difficult issues or life events.

The Mind Mental Health at Work website is a collection of work-related guides from mental health experts.
