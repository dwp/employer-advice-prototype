You might need to make changes for a disabled employee

A disabled employee should have the same opportunity to do their job as someone who is not disabled.

If your employee’s disability puts them at a substantial disadvantage, you might need to make changes to their role, environment, or the way they do their job.

These changes are called ‘reasonable adjustments’.
