Tips on talking to your employee

These topics can help you have an open and useful conversation with your employee.



Ask about them first

Start by asking about your employee in general - find out how they're doing.

"Thanks for letting me know..."

"How are you in general?"



Your employee's situation

You can start to ask about your employee's experience, and check anything you have already learned with them.

"What are you comfortable sharing right now?"

"Am I right in thinking this about your condition?"



Challenges at work

When it feels appropriate, talk about how the job or work environment might be making things difficult. You could arrange a time to talk about support in more detail.

"Which parts of work are making things difficult right now?"

"When you're ready we can talk about changes..."



Plan to keep in touch

Agree when you will get in touch next, and how often you will speak. Choose a method of communication that works for you both.

"When should we talk again?"

"What type of format works best for you for us to chat?"
