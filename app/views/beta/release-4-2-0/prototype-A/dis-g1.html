Your employee can take sick leave

If you cannot make changes to help your employee stay at work, they might benefit from some time off.

If you cannot identify any changes to help your employee, or the changes are not reasonable for your business, the situation may still improve over time.

Your employee can take sick leave to see if their condition improves.

After 7 days, you can ask for proof of their condition, like a Fit Note. If they stay off for over 4 weeks, they may be considered long-term sick.
