Checklist for deciding on changes at work

This checklist can help you decide on any changes you can make to support your employee. Working through the three areas together will help you to identify the right changes.



Changes to the environment

This might include things like desk space, equipment and noise levels. How can you make the work environment more suitable?

Example: someone who uses a wheelchair might need an accessible car parking space



Ways of working

Consider changes to hours, shift patterns or other policies that can help your employee do their best at work.

Example: if a condition affects someone's energy in the morning, they might need to start their working day later.



Extra help

It might help your employee to have someone to help with parts of their job or how they access work.

Example: if a condition affects someone's eyesight, they might need someone to read their post for them.



Check in with your employee as often as works for you both. You can update this sheet or start a new one.
