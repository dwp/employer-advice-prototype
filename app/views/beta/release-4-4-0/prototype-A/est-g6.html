What to do if your employee has not told you about a health condition or disability

If your employee is off work because of a health condition or disability, you should be considerate when asking questions about their absence. The employee might find it difficult to discuss the details.

It's important to create a culture where employees are happy to talk about their health.

Avoid judgements when you talk about disabilities or health conditions. This is especially important for mental health conditions.

Explain that you will keep health information private, and only use it to make changes to help them do their job.
