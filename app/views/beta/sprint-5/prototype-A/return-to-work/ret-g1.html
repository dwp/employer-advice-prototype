How to help your employee return to work when they’re ready

You should keep in contact with your employee. Staying in touch will make it easier for your employee to tell you if they feel ready to return to work.

Find more information about dismissing someone fairly on the ACAS website.

You might find it helpful to come back to this website when your employee has told you they are ready to return. You will be able to answer these questions again, and see guidance on what you should say and do to help your employee return to work.
