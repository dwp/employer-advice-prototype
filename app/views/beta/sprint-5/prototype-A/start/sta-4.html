Support employees with disabilities and health conditions

This service is for employers and managers. It will help you support an employee who:

  • tells you they have a long-term illness or disability
  • is off work sick
  • is ready to return to work after a long sickness absence

This service will help you understand your legal obligations regarding employee health and disability discrimination. You will also see advice to help you manage employees with health conditions.
