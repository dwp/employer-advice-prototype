Examples of changes for mental health conditions

Mental health conditions are all different, so no change will suit everyone.

You can ask your employee about possible changes like:

  • having quiet areas in the office (a change to the workplace)
  • different shift patterns or start times (a change to ways of working)
  • providing help to managing their workload (extra support)

More about reasonable adjustments on GOV.UK

Talking about mental health

Help with costs might be available if you cannot make the changes your employee needs.

Your employee might be worried about approaching you, or asking for changes. This can be especially hard for less visible conditions like mental illness.

You could ask your employee if there is anything you can do to make them more comfortable. Think about how you can create a culture where everyone is happy to talk about mental health.
