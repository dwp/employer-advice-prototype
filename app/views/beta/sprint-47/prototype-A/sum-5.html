Checking policies and keeping information private

There are three policies that businesses are required to have. These are:

  • Disciplinary and dismissal
  • Grievance
  • Health and safety (if you have more than five employees)

As a minimum, to protect the business and your employees, you should also consider having the following policies that relate to health and disability:

  • Equality, diversity and inclusion
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), which covers the management and processing of personal data
  • Sickness absence procedure
  • Dignity at work policy

You can adapt these templates from Acas.org.uk (opens in new tab) for your business

Check how to prepare a health and safety policy (opens in new tab) on the HSE website.

Remember that you need to keep a record of any discussions you have with your employee, but you must keep these private. As an employer, you have a duty of care to protect your employees.
